Principles
One accent, used with intent. Amber marks only what you can act on.
Safety reads instantly. Green for progress, red for SOS — never the brand hue.
Maps recede. Muted land, soft roads; the route and your car are the only bright things.
Numbers hold still. Tabular figures for fares, ETAs and plates.

Interactive design prototype · rendered live in CSS, no images · maps use OpenStreetMap-style tiling in production. Built to match the Taxi Platform brand and guide the Jetpack Compose build.
